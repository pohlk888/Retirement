Key Planning Notes

  • CPF LIFE Standard Plan is the preferred working assumption.
  • CPF LIFE payout is planned to be deferred until age 70.
  • Bridging income is needed before CPF LIFE starts.
  • OA, SRS, cash, and possible insurance surrender value are key bridging resources.
  • Do not double-count CPF SA and RA after age 55, because SA is used to form RA.
